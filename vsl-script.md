## Production Notes

### Visual Direction:
- Clean, professional background (dark blue/charcoal)
- Text overlays for key phrases
- Subtle motion graphics for "automation" concepts
- Screenshots of Open Claw interface (optional)
- Icons/graphics for problem/solution sections

### Tone:
- Clear and direct
- Professional but approachable
- Benefit-driven
- No hype or overpromising

### Music:
- Upbeat but not distracting
- Corporate/professional feel
- Builds toward CTA

### Length Target:
60-75 seconds ideal
90 seconds maximum
